# Ravn
### Your HYROX edge, in one app

*Native iOS · built on Apple Health · powered by an AI coach*

---

Most training apps hand you a generic plan and a step count. **Ravn figures out what's actually holding you back, builds you a plan to your race, and adjusts every day based on how recovered you are** — like having a coach in your pocket who knows your numbers.

---

### It finds your limiter
A quick baseline places you on the HYROX map — strong but heavy, fast but weak, sharp everywhere, or building from scratch. It reads your real numbers (your 5K, your lifts measured against *your division's* standards, your mobility) and tells you, plainly, the one thing costing you the most time.

### It builds a plan to race day
From your race date and your limiter, Ravn lays out a full periodized build — base, build, peak, taper — and fills in every week with real sessions. Not "go for a run." **Run paces calculated from your goal finish time. Station work at the exact weights you'll race.**

### It knows when to push and when to back off
Ravn reads your recovery from Apple Health — heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, sleep — and scores it against *your own* baseline, not a generic chart. Train hard yesterday? It knows. Getting sick? It catches the signal before you feel it. Every morning you get one honest number: how ready you are today.

### It coaches you
Ask it anything — "is my goal realistic?", "I feel wrecked, what should I do?", "rebuild my week around more strength" — and it answers from *your* data, and can change your plan on the spot. It won't guess off stale numbers; if it doesn't trust the data, it says so.

---

### Why it's different
- **It compares you to you.** Recovery is measured against your own baseline — the only comparison that means anything.
- **It's built for the race you're doing.** Strength, stations, and pacing all scale to your exact division.
- **It works backward from your goal.** Your target finish time sets your training paces — and tells you if you're chasing something unrealistic.
- **The coach actually does things.** It doesn't just talk; it rewrites your plan and goals on command.

---

**Ravn — train the limiter, not the routine.**
